Not Just Another
Ice Cream Shop.

Burnt Cones was born from an obsession with the perfect pairing. We realized that while gelato quality mattered, the vessel—the cone—was often an afterthought.

We pioneered the signature 12-shade cone spectrum, from buttery light to deep charcoal black, each designed to complement our low-sugar, high-intensity gelato recipes.
